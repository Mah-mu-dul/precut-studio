Brand Colors


  

1️⃣ Primary Color – Sky Blue #87CEEB
* Use for: Highlights, buttons, links, icons, hover states
* Feels: Fresh, modern, creative
________________


2️⃣ Secondary Color – Dark Navy #091549
* Use for: Backgrounds, headers, footers, main text on light areas, bold sections
* Feels: Premium, trustworthy, strong contrast
________________


3️⃣ Neutral / Base – Beige #F5F5DC
* Use for: Backgrounds, cards, section fills, secondary elements
* Feels: Warm, soft, balances bold blues

4️⃣ Accent / Gradient
   * Combine Sky Blue → Dark Navy for hover effects, CTA buttons, or subtle gradients
   * Example gradient: linear-gradient(135deg, #87CEEB 0%, #091549 100%)
________________


5️⃣ Typography Colors
   * Main text: Dark Navy (#091549)
   * Secondary text / highlights: Sky Blue (#87CEEB) or Beige (#F5F5DC) depending on background

Brand Fonts




Primary: Space Mono
Secondary:  DM Sans Medium





Examples:
    
Font Usage Recommendation
1️⃣ Primary Font – Space Mono
      * Use for: Headlines, hero section, titles, navigation, CTA buttons
      * Why: Monospaced, geometric, modern — gives personality and makes your brand feel creative and distinctive
      * Bold, techy, and attention-grabbing; perfect for your key brand statements
________________
2️⃣ Secondary Font – DM Sans Medium
         * Use for: Body text, subheadings, captions, descriptions, small labels
         * Why: Highly readable, minimal, and balances the more rigid Space Mono
         * Clean, readable, versatile; complements Space Mono without competing for attention
