Why This Is Strong
It:
         * Feels cinematic
         * Creates brand memory
         * Makes user participate (scroll to enter)
         * Builds anticipation

But it doesn’t waste time.
Total duration:
            * 2–4 seconds max depending on scroll speed.
Very important:
Allow users to skip.
Options:
            * “Skip Intro” small text
OR
            * If user scrolls fast → immediately transition

Never trap users.Luxury is smooth, not forced.
